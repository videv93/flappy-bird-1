Direction 1: Cozy Minimal

Clean, generous whitespace with Instagram-like simplicity. Emphasizes calm and belonging through spacious layout.

Strengths

  • Maximum calm feeling
  • Easy to scan
  • Mobile-first native
  • Focus on one book

Considerations

  • Less info density
  • More scrolling needed
  • Simple may feel empty

Direction 2: Dense Library

Grid-based layout showing more books at once. StoryGraph-like information density with warm colors.

Strengths

  • See more books
  • Efficient for power users
  • Stats-forward
  • Library feel

Considerations

  • Less calm feeling
  • Smaller touch targets
  • Presence less visible

Direction 3: Feed-Forward

Activity feed as the primary view. Social-first approach like Twitter/Strava with reading sessions in timeline.

Strengths

  • Maximizes social
  • Familiar pattern
  • Discovery-friendly
  • Engagement-driven

Considerations

  • Less book-focused
  • May feel noisy
  • Reading rooms secondary
